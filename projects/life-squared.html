LIFE-SQUARED

Life-Squared or Life2 is a campaign aimed at raising awareness for organ donation to help with research in regenerative bio-technology in organ transplantation.

Life2 uses print and digital marketing strategies targeting audiences of all age groups to motivate organ donors to be a part of the next big step in medicine.

I worked on print designs such as complimentary gift bags, T-Shirts and social media advertisements that would be a part of the campaign. The concept of social media advertisements would snag the highest amount of audience and the complimentary items provided motivation for attendace at conventions hosted by the campaign.
